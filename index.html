about the pod

Join host Kass as she delves into the lives of nomads, adventurers, and those who have chosen unconventional paths.
Each episode offers a unique perspective and inspiring tale through cultural insights and personal journeys. Whether you're a seasoned traveler or simply curious about the world, A Backpacker's Diary will take you on a captivating adventure.
